## SURA Project 2: Data Driven Measures of CXT Learning Outcomes

### Contextual Thinking (CXT)

Trains students to consider the circumstances surrounding issues, objects
Our students will learn to explain why and how problems persist and what attempts have been made to solve them

### Using student data from CXT courses, the project will: 
- Help faculty make decisions about assignments, rubrics, and course design
- Help administrators within Dietrich College planning the program for future students
- Investigate whether all students, regardless of background or major, or other factors are gaining equal value from CXT courses

### CXT Learning Outcomes
- CXT 1: Question
- CXT 2a: Quality of Evidence
- CXT 2b: Use of Evidence
- CXT 3: Historical Analysis
- CXT 4a: Traditions of Thought
- CXT 4b: Reflection
